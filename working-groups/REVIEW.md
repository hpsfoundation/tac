# Working Group Review Template

## Information About the Working Group
* What is the purpose of the working group?
* What problems are you trying to solve?
* What are the main goals of the working group?

## Statistics
* How often do you meet?
* How many people attend on average?
* Do you keep notes from the meetings?
* What topics have been covered so far?
* Which projects have engaged so far?

## Progress
* What goals have you achieved?
* What can the TAC do to help?

## Future Goals
* What are your current priorities?
* What are the initiatives and activities to support these priorities?
